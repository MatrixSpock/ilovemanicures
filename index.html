Full Set (Short $40+ / Long $45+)

Tip and acrylic powder enhancement with ONE solid color of your choice.

Overlay ($35)

Acrylic is applied over NATURAL nail plate. There is no added length, but nails are less likely to crack, peel or break, and the polish lasts longer. Finish with ONE solid color of your choice.

Fill ($35+)

Acrylic over existing acrylic nails with ONE solid color of your choice.

French (Tips $45+ / Hand-drawn $55+)

French Tips can be done with a tip or a hand drawn. French with tips is done by placing a white tip and placing clear acrylic over the tip. Hand-drawn French tips are drawn onto a full set with a gel color of your choice.

Ombre Nails ($50)

Acrylic powder usually pink and white graduating into each other making a smooth blended look.
